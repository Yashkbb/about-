About ecomeasy

Hum kaun hain

ecomeasy ek online education platform hai jo logon ko Meesho, Flipkart, Amazon jaise e-commerce marketplaces par apna online selling business shuru karna sikhata hai. Hamara maqsad simple hai — bina kisi bhaari background ya investment ke, kisi bhi insaan ko ghar baithe online seller banne layak banana.

Hum video-based, Hindi mein step-by-step training dete hain jisme account setup se lekar pehli sale tak ka poora process cover hota hai.

Hamari Shuruaat

ecomeasy ke founder ne khud 2022 se online selling ki hai aur trial-and-error se yeh seekha ki ek naya seller kis tarah ki galtiyan karta hai. Isi experience se yeh course banaya gaya, taaki naye sellers ko wahi mushkilein dobara na jhelni padein.

Hamara Mission

Har student ko practical, real-world skills dena jo unhe genuinely online business start karne mein madad karein — sirf theory nahi, balki actual screen-recordings, templates aur calculators ke saath.
